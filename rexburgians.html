About

Rexburgians is an app that is designed to make planning dates easier, from searching good recipes for your date to planning where to go, Rexburgians is the app just for you!

Rexburgians will be able do any of the following:

  • Search for, rate, and post your own dating ideas on our date ideas forum
  • Search for good places to take your date to
  • Check the weather to make sure you aren't picnicking in the rain
  • Pick a random recipe to try out something new on your date
  • Hang with your friends on our new hangout forum
  • Plan out your date by checking the BYUI Event Calendar
  • And much more!

Rexburgians is still being made for the PC and Android tablet; to show your support you can give us ideas or code on our GitHub page.
